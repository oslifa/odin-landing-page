Walking

It's something that makes us human. But, in a world of seated transport and at-home conveniences, it's become an increasingly forgotten activity.

You may consider this a minor loss. But walking is much more than simply a way to get from A to B.

Why should I walk?

Boost your physical fitness and health

Boost your mood and cognitive function

Reduce your ecological footprint

Reduce your travel expenses

“But the beauty is in the walking — we are betrayed by destinations.”

Gwyn Thomas

Try walking

Next time you need to visit your local shops, meet with friends, travel to school or work, or have time to spare, consider how taking a walk will benefit you.
