Walking

It's something that makes us human. But, in a world of seated transport and services provided at home, it's becoming an increasingly forgotten activity.

You may consider this a minor loss. But walking can be much more than simply a way to get from A to B.

Why walk?

Boost your physical fitness and health.

Boost your cognitive functioning and mood.

Minimise the ecological footprint of your trips.

Minimise your travel expenses.

“But the beauty is in the walking — we are betrayed by destinations.”

— Gwyn Thomas

Try walking

Next time you need to visit your local shops, meet with friends, travel to school or work, or have time to spare, consider how taking a walk will benefit you.
